Data Management

Importing and Exporting Data

Salesforce administrators can import data into Salesforce from external sources or export data from Salesforce for backup or analysis purposes. This process is facilitated by data import/export tools such as Data Loader or Salesforce's data import wizard.

Custom Objects and Fields

Administrators can create custom objects and fields to tailor Salesforce to their organization's specific needs. Custom objects represent different types of records, while custom fields capture additional information specific to those records. This allows for the storage and management of unique data elements beyond the standard Salesforce objects.

Record Types, Page Layouts, and List Views

Record types define the various configurations of a particular object, allowing for different sets of picklist values, page layouts, and business processes. Page layouts determine the arrangement of fields and related lists on record detail pages. List views provide customizable ways to filter and display sets of records based on specific criteria.

Data Quality and Maintenance

Administrators are responsible for ensuring data quality and integrity within Salesforce. This involves regular data cleanup activities, such as identifying and merging duplicate records, updating outdated information, and enforcing data validation rules.

Data Security and Compliance

Admins implement data security measures to protect sensitive information and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements. This includes setting up sharing rules, field-level security, object-level security, and encryption to control access to data and prevent unauthorized usage or exposure.
