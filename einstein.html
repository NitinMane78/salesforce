Analytics & Einstein

Reporting

Salesforce Reporting allows users to analyze their data and generate actionable insights using customizable reports:

  • Standard & Custom Reports: Salesforce provides a library of standard reports covering common use cases, as well as the ability to create custom reports tailored to specific business requirements.
  • Drag-and-Drop Interface: Users can easily build reports using a drag-and-drop interface, selecting fields, filters, and grouping options to visualize their data in tables, charts, or matrices.
  • Filters & Summaries: Reports can be filtered to focus on specific criteria or time periods, and users can add summary formulas to calculate totals, averages, or other aggregate metrics.
  • Scheduled & Automated Reporting: Users can schedule reports to run at predefined intervals and automatically distribute them via email or Salesforce Chatter, ensuring stakeholders receive timely insights.

Dashboards

Salesforce Dashboards enable users to visualize and monitor key metrics and KPIs in real-time, providing actionable insights at a glance:

  • Dynamic Dashboards: Users can create dynamic dashboards consisting of multiple components, such as charts, tables, and metrics, to track various aspects of their business performance.
  • Interactive Filters: Dashboards can include interactive filters that allow users to dynamically adjust the data displayed based on criteria such as date range, region, or product category.
  • Drill-Down Capabilities: Users can drill down into dashboard components to explore underlying data in more detail, facilitating deeper analysis and investigation of trends or outliers.
  • Mobile Access: Dashboards are accessible on mobile devices through the Salesforce mobile app, enabling users to stay informed and make data-driven decisions on the go.

Einstein Analytics

Einstein Analytics empowers organizations with AI-driven insights and predictive analytics capabilities to unlock the full potential of their data:

  • Advanced Analytics: Einstein Analytics leverages AI and machine learning algorithms to uncover hidden patterns, trends, and correlations within large datasets, enabling organizations to make data-backed decisions.
  • Predictive Insights: Einstein Discovery enables users to build predictive models and forecasts based on historical data, identifying factors that drive outcomes and recommending actions to optimize results.
  • Smart Data Preparation: Einstein Data Prep automates data cleansing, transformation, and enrichment tasks, reducing manual effort and ensuring data quality for analysis.
  • Embedded Analytics: Einstein Analytics seamlessly integrates with Salesforce CRM and other data sources, embedding analytics directly into business processes and workflows for actionable insights where users need them most.

AI-driven Insights

Salesforce Einstein delivers AI-driven insights across the Salesforce platform to drive smarter decisions and enhance user productivity:

  • Lead Scoring & Opportunity Insights: Einstein Lead Scoring and Opportunity Insights leverage AI to prioritize leads and opportunities based on their likelihood to convert, empowering sales teams to focus on high-value prospects.
  • Service & Support Intelligence: Einstein Case Classification and Service Analytics analyze support cases to identify trends, recommend solutions, and optimize service processes for improved customer satisfaction.
  • Marketing Personalization: Einstein Engagement Scoring and Predictive Audiences enable marketers to personalize campaigns, predict customer behavior, and optimize engagement strategies to drive higher conversion rates and ROI.
  • Community & Commerce Insights: Einstein Community Insights and Commerce Insights provide actionable analytics for community managers and commerce teams, enabling them to optimize community engagement and drive revenue growth.
