Automation

Workflows

Workflows allow Salesforce administrators to automate standard business processes and streamline operations. With workflows, you can define rules that trigger automatic actions, such as sending email alerts, updating field values, or creating tasks.

Approval Processes

Approval processes automate the approval of records in Salesforce. Administrators can define criteria for record approval and set up multiple steps with assigned approvers. This helps enforce business rules and ensures proper authorization for critical actions.

Process Builder

Process Builder is a powerful automation tool that allows administrators to build complex, multi-step processes without writing code. With Process Builder, you can automate repetitive tasks, update multiple related records, and trigger actions based on specific criteria.

Flows

Flows are similar to Process Builder but offer even more flexibility and customization options. With Flows, you can create guided visual experiences for users, automate complex business processes, and integrate with external systems.

Scheduled Actions

Scheduled actions allow administrators to schedule automatic actions to occur at specific times or intervals. This feature is useful for tasks such as sending reminders, updating records on a recurring basis, or triggering follow-up actions based on predefined schedules.
