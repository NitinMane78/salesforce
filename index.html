Welcome to Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) platform that was developed by Marc Benioff and Parker Harris in 1999. It was created with the vision of revolutionizing the way businesses interact with their customers and manage their operations.

Over the years, Salesforce has grown to become the world's leading CRM platform, serving millions of users across various industries. It offers a wide range of products and services, including Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Commerce Cloud, and more, helping businesses of all sizes drive growth and innovation.

With its innovative technology, user-friendly interface, and robust ecosystem of apps and integrations, Salesforce continues to empower organizations to build stronger relationships with their customers, streamline their processes, and accelerate their success in the digital age.

Historical Background

Salesforce was founded in 1999 in San Francisco, California, with a mission to reinvent CRM for the digital age. Marc Benioff and Parker Harris, along with a small team of developers, launched the first version of Salesforce as a cloud-based CRM solution.

In the years that followed, Salesforce experienced rapid growth and expansion, introducing new products, acquiring companies, and establishing itself as a leader in the tech industry. Today, Salesforce is a global company with thousands of employees and millions of users worldwide.

Throughout its history, Salesforce has remained committed to its core values of trust, innovation, customer success, and equality. It continues to innovate and evolve, empowering businesses to connect with their customers in entirely new ways.
